SYNDICATED LOAN AGREEMENT

This Loan Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 1, 2024, by and among:

BORROWER: TechCorp Industries, Inc., a Delaware corporation
LENDERS: FirstBank Capital, LLC and Regional Finance Partners, LP
AGENT: FirstBank Capital, LLC, as Administrative Agent

LOAN AMOUNT: $50,000,000 (Fifty Million Dollars)
INTEREST RATE: SOFR + 2.50% per annum
MATURITY DATE: December 31, 2028

ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS AND TERMS

1.1 Credit Facility: The Lenders agree to provide a revolving credit facility in the aggregate principal amount not to exceed $50,000,000.

1.2 Use of Proceeds: The Borrower shall use the proceeds of the Loans solely for working capital and general corporate purposes.

ARTICLE 2: CONDITIONS PRECEDENT

2.1 Initial Funding: The obligation of each Lender to make the initial Loan is subject to receipt of executed loan documents and evidence of insurance coverage.

ARTICLE 3: REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

3.1 Organization: The Borrower is duly organized, validly existing, and in good standing under the laws of Delaware.

3.2 Financial Condition: The financial statements delivered to the Lenders fairly present the financial condition of the Borrower.

3.3 No Material Adverse Change: There has been no material adverse change in the business, operations, or financial condition of the Borrower.

ARTICLE 4: AFFIRMATIVE COVENANTS

The Borrower covenants and agrees that, so long as any Loan remains outstanding:

4.1 Financial Statements: The Borrower shall deliver to the Agent:
    (a) Annual audited financial statements within 90 days of fiscal year end
    (b) Quarterly unaudited financial statements within 45 days of quarter end
    (c) Monthly management reports within 30 days of month end

4.2 Compliance Certificate: Together with each delivery of financial statements, the Borrower shall provide a compliance certificate certifying compliance with all covenants.

4.3 Insurance: The Borrower shall maintain insurance coverage on all properties and assets with reputable insurance companies in amounts and types customary for similar businesses.

4.4 Existence: The Borrower shall preserve and maintain its corporate existence, rights, franchises, and privileges.

4.5 Compliance with Laws: The Borrower shall comply in all material respects with all applicable federal, state, and local laws and regulations.

4.6 Books and Records: The Borrower shall maintain proper books of record and account in accordance with GAAP.

ARTICLE 5: NEGATIVE COVENANTS

The Borrower covenants and agrees that, so long as any Loan remains outstanding, without prior written consent of the Required Lenders:

5.1 Indebtedness: The Borrower shall not create, incur, assume, or permit to exist any Indebtedness, except:
    (a) Indebtedness under this Agreement
    (b) Existing Indebtedness disclosed in Schedule 5.1
    (c) Additional unsecured Indebtedness not to exceed $5,000,000 in the aggregate

5.2 Liens: The Borrower shall not create, incur, assume, or permit to exist any Lien on any of its properties or assets, except for Permitted Liens as defined.

5.3 Investments: The Borrower shall not make any Investment, except:
    (a) Investments in cash equivalents
    (b) Investments in wholly-owned subsidiaries not to exceed $2,000,000 annually

5.4 Restricted Payments: The Borrower shall not declare or pay any dividend or make any distribution on account of its equity interests, except:
    (a) Dividends payable solely in equity interests
    (b) Cash dividends not to exceed $1,000,000 per fiscal year if no Default exists

5.5 Asset Sales: The Borrower shall not sell, transfer, lease, or otherwise dispose of any assets except in the ordinary course of business.

5.6 Fundamental Changes: The Borrower shall not merge, consolidate, liquidate, or dissolve, or permit any Subsidiary to do so.

ARTICLE 6: FINANCIAL COVENANTS

The Borrower covenants and agrees to maintain, tested quarterly:

6.1 Minimum Debt Service Coverage Ratio: The Borrower shall maintain a Debt Service Coverage Ratio of not less than 1.25 to 1.00, tested quarterly on a rolling four-quarter basis.

    Debt Service Coverage Ratio = (EBITDA) / (Interest Expense + Principal Payments)

6.2 Maximum Leverage Ratio: The Borrower shall not permit the Leverage Ratio to exceed 3.50 to 1.00, tested quarterly.

    Leverage Ratio = (Total Funded Debt) / (EBITDA for the trailing four fiscal quarters)

6.3 Minimum Current Ratio: The Borrower shall maintain a Current Ratio of not less than 1.10 to 1.00, tested quarterly.

    Current Ratio = (Current Assets) / (Current Liabilities)

6.4 Minimum EBITDA: The Borrower shall maintain minimum EBITDA of not less than $10,000,000 for any trailing four fiscal quarter period.

ARTICLE 7: EVENTS OF DEFAULT

7.1 Payment Default: Failure to pay principal when due, or failure to pay interest or fees within five days of when due.

7.2 Covenant Default: Failure to comply with any covenant in Articles 4, 5, or 6, and such failure continues for 30 days after written notice.

7.3 Representation Default: Any representation or warranty proves to be materially incorrect when made.

7.4 Cross-Default: Default under any other Indebtedness exceeding $1,000,000.

7.5 Bankruptcy: The Borrower becomes subject to any bankruptcy or insolvency proceeding.

7.6 Material Adverse Change: Any material adverse change in the business, operations, properties, or financial condition of the Borrower.

ARTICLE 8: REMEDIES

Upon occurrence of an Event of Default, the Lenders may:
- Declare all outstanding Loans immediately due and payable
- Terminate all commitments to extend further credit
- Exercise any and all rights and remedies available at law or in equity

ARTICLE 9: REPORTING OBLIGATIONS

9.1 Quarterly Reporting: Within 45 days of each fiscal quarter end, the Borrower shall provide:
    - Unaudited quarterly financial statements
    - Compliance certificate with covenant calculations
    - Management discussion and analysis

9.2 Annual Reporting: Within 90 days of fiscal year end, the Borrower shall provide:
    - Audited annual financial statements
    - Annual compliance certificate
    - Annual budget and projections for upcoming year

9.3 Prompt Notification: The Borrower shall promptly notify the Agent of:
    - Any Event of Default or potential Default
    - Any litigation or governmental proceeding exceeding $500,000
    - Any material adverse change in business or operations

ARTICLE 10: MISCELLANEOUS

10.1 Governing Law: This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.

10.2 Amendments: This Agreement may be amended only by written instrument signed by the Borrower and Required Lenders.

10.3 Notices: All notices shall be in writing and delivered to the addresses specified.
